<Update date="2026-05-12" title="Integrate Prompty-based chat with Azure OpenAI, embeddings, and semantic search">
Adds a Prompty-powered chat flow for Contoso Chat: loads environment variables, initializes tracing, retrieves customer data from Cosmos DB, derives product context via Prompty, and executes the chat model through Azure OpenAI. Introduces embeddings-based product discovery and semantic search: generate_embeddings creates embeddings for queries, and retrieve_products queries contoso-products with Azure Cognitive Search. Orchestrates product discovery in find_products by calling product.prompty, then embedding and search steps. Wires up Prompty-based tracing across chat and evaluators to enable end-to-end telemetry, enabling coherence, relevance, fluency, and groundedness evaluation.
